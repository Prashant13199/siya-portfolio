About Us

SIYA is how people build softwares


Established in 2018 Siya is an open source organization for helping and nurturing beginners. We’re starting a community where people can learn, share, and work together to build software. Come help us make collaboration even better.

We’ve built a company we truly love working for, and we think you will too. Here you can get free access to various project ideas and their implementations.

We’re working hard to build a supportive, welcoming place for users and coders.
